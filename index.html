Creative Club

Vision: To foster a vibrant literary and creative culture among students by providing platforms that encourage original thinking, expressive storytelling, critical dialogue, and impactful performance.

Mission: Blending words and pixels to spark creativity, expression, and innovation.

About the Club

Traditional Expression

Dive into the timeless world of storytelling, poetry, and spoken word. Our workshops and open mics are a stage for your voice, where classic artistry meets a modern audience. We celebrate the power of the pen and the passion of the performance.

Digital Creativity

Explore the frontier of digital art. From short film challenges and podcast sessions to graphic design and meme creation, we empower you to translate your creative vision to the screen. We believe that pixels can be just as powerful as prose.

What We Do

Expressive On-the-Spot

Draw a random prompt and perform an original piece in 3 minutes. A thrilling test of spontaneity and creativity.

Story Fragment Challenge

Use assorted fragments like images and notes to collaboratively craft a logical and engaging story against the clock.

Short Film Creation

Take complete creative control. Brainstorm, script, shoot, and edit an original short film with your team from concept to final cut.

AI Story-to-Comic

Blend writing and technology. Write a short narrative and use AI tools to generate visuals, transforming your story into a comic strip.
